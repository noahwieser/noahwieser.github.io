Data And Deletion Help

LiftLog is local-first and does not require an account.

  • To remove workout templates and history in-app: Workouts tab -> More actions (three dots) -> Delete all workouts.
  • To remove remaining local app data: uninstall LiftLog from your device.

Known Scope

  • Data is stored locally on your device for this release.
  • There is no cloud backup/sync provided by LiftLog in this release.
