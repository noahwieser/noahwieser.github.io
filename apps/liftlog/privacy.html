LiftLog

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

LiftLog is a local-first app. Your workout and profile data stays on your device unless you choose to share it yourself.

Data Stored On Device

LiftLog stores data locally using on-device storage (SQLite and app settings), including workout templates, exercises, set plans, completed workout history, one-rep max entries, app settings, and optional profile values (for example name, age, height, and weight).

Data We Do Not Collect Or Send

  • No account is required.
  • We do not send workout or profile data to LiftLog servers.
  • We do not use cloud sync for this release.
  • We do not sell personal information.

Third Parties And Tracking

  • LiftLog does not include ad SDKs.
  • LiftLog does not include third-party analytics SDKs.
  • LiftLog does not track you across apps or websites.

iOS Live Activities Disclosure

If you use LiftLog on iOS with Live Activities enabled, active workout details (such as workout name and next set information) may appear on the lock screen or Dynamic Island. This information remains on-device but may be visible to someone who can view your screen.

Data Retention And Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete workout templates/history in-app, and uninstalling the app may remove remaining local app data.

Children's Privacy

LiftLog is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the last updated date.
